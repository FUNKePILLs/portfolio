Graphic Design

I've been using Photoshop and Illustrator for a bit over a year now, and I'm quite skilled at it, and I'm learning more.

I can retouch photos, focus on subjects, take objects out of a picture, crop and mask, feather, and modify in Photoshop. I use Illustrator for graphic design as it is raster-based, meaning I can work with images of any scale without quality loss. Illustrator is very flexible and I've got up to a point where I can maintain a good workflow and be quick in my projects whilst maintaining good quality.

I've put time into learning both After Effects and Premiere Pro, wherein I can create video compostions, like advertisements, and informative videos.

I've created graphic and video projects for a cryptocurrency startup back in 2018 where I designed their logos and website banners, and participated in video essay competitions where I made short ad videos about their cryptocurrency, LBC. I was compensated well for my works.
